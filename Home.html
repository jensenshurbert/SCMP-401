Welcome to the Belize Solar Project

Have you been thinking about installing solar energy to your home, work place or school? Have you already installed solar panels through this project and wish to see the status of how your systems are working? Well, you've come to the right place!

Solar power is a great source of energy that can significantly reduce your carbon footprint and your investment can even pay off in the end. Since the introduction of solar panels, they have become much more popular, which has driven the price much lower than the original. If you are deciding whether solar energy is for you, take a look at our website and decide for yourself!

Here you can learn more about our project, the importance and impact of solar energy, and the direct impact the solar systems we have installed have had so far.
