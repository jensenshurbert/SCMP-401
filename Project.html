The Work We Do

Every spring break, Professor Robert Alexander and Professor James Skon take a team of students from Kenyon College to Belize to work on building Grid-Tie Solar Systems for several Schools. This service project includes learning about solar power systems, working with Belize schools, and seeing Belize and experiencing it's culture, environment, history, and people.

With the addition of these solar power systems, students are able to have access to new computer labs which are powered by the sun. Not only does this provide a great learning experience, but also can turn an investment into something that will save money down the road. Explore our webpage to learn more about what we do, and to view the solar data live!
