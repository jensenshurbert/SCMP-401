Solar Energy

Solar power is the conversion of energy from the sun into a usable energy source, such as electricity. It is a renewable free source of energy that is much more sustainable than our current resources. Fossil fuels are not only bad for the environment but they are also a resource that will not last forever. With the addition of solar energy, we have a new source of energy that is non-polluting and doesn't emit greenhouse gases.

Our team puts in grid-tied solar systems, which are systems that are connected to the electrical grid so it uses electricity from both the solar panel system and the electrical grid. Below is a diagram of how Solar Grid-tied systems work.

Our work specifically aims to help primary schools in Belize with their power sources. With the introduction of many new computer labs, solar power can provide these schools with affordable energy. There is a common belief that solar panels are a very large investment, but these investments will pay off. Soon to come is our team's analysis on the cost efficiency and how soon our systems will begin making money off their initial investment.
